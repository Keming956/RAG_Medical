The Journal of Social and Clinical Psychology is a bimonthly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering social and clinical psychology. It was established in 1983 by John Harvey and is published by Guilford Press. The editor-in-chief is Thomas Joiner (Florida State University). According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2019 impact factor of 1.472.
