Progress in Neuro-Psychopharmacology & Biological Psychiatry is a peer-reviewed academic journal publishing review articles and original research reports pertaining to neuropsychopharmacology and biological psychiatry. It was established in 1977 as Progress in Neuro-Psychopharmacology, obtaining its current name in 1982. It is published eight times per year by Elsevier.
