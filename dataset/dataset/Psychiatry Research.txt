Psychiatry Research is a peer-reviewed medical journal covering psychiatry. It was established in 1979 and is published 21 times per year by Elsevier. A section of the journal, Psychiatry Research: Neuroimaging, covers the discipline of neuroimaging as it pertains to psychiatry. The editor-in-chief is Dr. Lynn DeLisi. According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2018 impact factor of 2.208.
