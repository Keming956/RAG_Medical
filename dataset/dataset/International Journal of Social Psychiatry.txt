The International Journal of Social Psychiatry is a peer-reviewed academic journal that covers research in the field of social psychiatry. The journal's editor-in-chief is Dinesh Bhugra (King's College London). It was established in 1955 and is currently published by Sage Publications.

Abstracting and indexing 
The International Journal of Social Psychiatry is abstracted and indexed in: Academic Search Premier, Current Contents, Family Index Database, International Nursing Index, PubMed: MEDLINE, Scopus, and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2017 impact factor is 1.613, ranking it 84th out of 142 journals in the category ‘Psychiatry’ (under SSCI - Social Sciences Citation Index).
