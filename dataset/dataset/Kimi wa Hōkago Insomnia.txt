is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Makoto Ojiro. It has been serialized in Shogakukan's seinen manga magazine Weekly Big Comic Spirits since May 2019. An anime television series adaptation and a live-action film adaptation have been announced.

Media

Manga
Written and illustrated by , Kimi wa Hōkago Insomnia started in Shogakukan's seinen manga magazine Weekly Big Comic Spirits on May 20, 2019. Shogakukan collected its chapters into individual tankōbon volumes. The first volume was released on September 12, 2019. As of January 12, 2022, eight volumes have been released.
